Favorite Movie

The Great Gatsby (2013)

My favorite movie is The Great Gatsby from 2013. The reason why this is my favorite movie is because of this movie has a modernized storytelling version from the original 1925 novel by American writer F. Scott Fitzgerald . In addition, this movie has 2 of my favorite actors - Leonardo DiCaprio and Tobey Maguire! I recommend if anyone is having a movie night, The Great Gatsby should be on their movie watch list!
